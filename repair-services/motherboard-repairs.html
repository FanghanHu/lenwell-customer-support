MOTHERBOARD REPAIRS

Computer's motherboard (also called a mainboard) is the central part of a computer. It is a complicated piece of circuitry that forms the main hub of your computer. Everything A computer does, from working with peripherals to using memory, to sending data to the monitor, is done through the motherboard. A faulty motherboard can completely halt the computer, or cause all sort of unexpected problems.

Many PC owners think they need to buy a new computer when their PC's motherboard is faulty. But in fact, it can be easily repaired or simply replaced. While it is the most important part of a computer, it is not expensive to repair or replace. By repairing your PC, not only you will be saving money, you will also help the planet by reducing electronic waste.
